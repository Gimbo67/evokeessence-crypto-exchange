## **Step 1: Transform Rule to Inject Headers**
Since scanners **don't see your `Content-Security-Policy` or `Permissions-Policy`** (even though your server sets them), Cloudflare may be stripping them or not passing them through for static files like `/favicon.ico`.

Here’s how to **force-add those headers for all responses** via Cloudflare:

### **Instructions (Transform Rule)**

1. **Go to** `Rules > Transform Rules` in Cloudflare Dashboard.
2. Click **“Create Rule”**.
3. Choose **“HTTP Response Header Modification”**.
4. **Name the rule**: `Inject Security Headers`
5. **Apply rule to**:
   - **Field**: `Hostname`
   - **Operator**: `equals`
   - **Value**: `evo-exchange.com`
6. Click **“Add Header Modification”**:
   - **Action**: `Set static`
   - **Header name**: `Content-Security-Policy`
   - **Value**:
     ```
     default-src 'self'; script-src 'self'; style-src 'self' 'unsafe-inline'; img-src 'self' data: blob:; font-src 'self' data:; connect-src 'self' wss: ws:; frame-src 'none'; object-src 'none'; base-uri 'none'; form-action 'self'; frame-ancestors 'none'; manifest-src 'self'; worker-src 'self' blob:; upgrade-insecure-requests;
     ```

7. **Add another Header Modification**:
   - **Action**: `Set static`
   - **Header name**: `Permissions-Policy`
   - **Value**:
     ```
     accelerometer=(), camera=(), geolocation=(), gyroscope=(), magnetometer=(), microphone=(), payment=(), usb=(), interest-cohort=()
     ```

8. Save and deploy the rule.

---

## **Step 2: Purge Cache**
After applying that rule:

1. Go to **Caching > Configuration**.
2. Click **Purge Everything**.

Why: This ensures all old cached responses are flushed and your new headers are applied to every file.

---

## **Then Test Again**
After this is done, recheck your site:

- [https://securityheaders.com](https://securityheaders.com)
- [https://observatory.mozilla.org](https://observatory.mozilla.org)

You should now see `Content-Security-Policy` and `Permissions-Policy` listed under your **raw headers** and score will increase to A or A+.
