Responsible Disclosure Policy

At evo-exchange.com, we take security seriously and value the contributions of ethical hackers and security researchers who help us protect our platform, our users, and their assets.

We encourage the responsible disclosure of security vulnerabilities and are committed to working with the security community to verify, reproduce, and respond to legitimate security issues.

Reporting Security Vulnerabilities

If you discover a vulnerability, please report it to us immediately at security@evo-exchange.com.

Please include the following information in your report:

  • A detailed description of the vulnerability
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Any proof-of-concept code if applicable
  • Potential impact of the vulnerability
  • Your name and contact information (if you wish to be credited)

What You Are Allowed to Test

You may test:

  • Public-facing API endpoints
  • Web application vulnerabilities (e.g., XSS, CSRF, injection flaws)
  • Authentication, session, and access control issues
  • Cryptographic weaknesses
  • Security misconfigurations

Rules of Engagement

Do NOT engage in the following:

  • Denial of Service (DoS) or Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks
  • Spam or automated scanning that generates significant traffic
  • Social engineering of our staff or users
  • Accessing, modifying, or destroying user data without permission
  • Physical attacks against our offices or data centers
  • Testing on production systems that could degrade service quality for our users

Our Commitment

When you submit a vulnerability report, we commit to:

  • Acknowledge receipt of your report within 48 hours
  • Provide a timeline for when we expect to resolve the issue
  • Notify you when the vulnerability has been fixed
  • Not take legal action against you if you follow this policy

Recognition

We're happy to recognize and acknowledge security researchers who help us improve our security. With your permission, we will add your name to our security acknowledgements page.

Depending on the severity and impact of the vulnerability, we may also offer:

  • Recognition in our security hall of fame
  • Public acknowledgement of your contribution (if you wish)
  • Reference letter confirming your contribution

Legal Safe Harbor

We will not pursue legal action against individuals who:

  • Make a good faith effort to avoid privacy violations, destruction of data, and disruption of our services
  • Only interact with accounts they own or with explicit permission from the account owner
  • Do not exploit a security issue for purposes other than verification
  • Report any vulnerability directly to us before disclosing it to the public

Disclosure Policy

We follow a coordinated vulnerability disclosure process:

  • Reports are handled confidentially, with details only shared as needed for resolution
  • Vulnerabilities will only be disclosed publicly after they have been resolved
  • We will work with you on the timing and content of any public disclosure

By submitting a report, you agree to handle all data and findings with confidentiality until we have resolved the issue.
