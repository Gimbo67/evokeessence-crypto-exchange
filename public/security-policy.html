Responsible Disclosure Policy

At evo-exchange.com, we take security seriously and value the contributions of ethical hackers and researchers who help us protect our platform, our users, and their assets.

Reporting Security Vulnerabilities

If you discover a vulnerability, please report it to us immediately at support@evo-exchange.com.

We encourage responsible disclosure and will work with you to verify and resolve the issue promptly.

What You Are Allowed to Test

You may test:

  • Public-facing API endpoints
  • Web application vulnerabilities (e.g., XSS, CSRF, injection flaws)
  • Authentication, session, and access control issues

Rules of Engagement

Do NOT engage in the following:

  • DDoS or spam attacks
  • Social engineering of our staff or users
  • Accessing or modifying user data without permission
  • Any physical or in-person attack vectors
  • Use of automated scanners that generate high traffic

Recognition

We're happy to recognize and acknowledge responsible researchers who help us improve security. Please let us know if you'd like to be credited on our acknowledgements page.

Legal

We will not pursue legal action against individuals who report vulnerabilities responsibly and follow this policy. By submitting a report, you agree to handle all data and findings with confidentiality.
